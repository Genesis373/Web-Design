Black Rhinoceros

Black Rhino Gallery

People everywhere love black rhinos, and they often support efforts to preserve them through many generous donations to foundations aimed at regrowing their population. Black rhinos also make great attractions, and tourists will most often go to wildlife reserves and zoos where they are being kept to see the magnificent creatures.

Black rhinos can be found on many reserves in Africa. A well-known reserve where they live is the Black Rhino Game Reserve in Pilanesberg, South Africa. One of the biggest national parks with black rhinoceroses as wildlife residents is the Serengeti National Park in Tanzania. The largest black rhino sanctuary in the world is the Ol Pejeta Widelife Reserve, located in Kenya.

Several zoos across the United States have black rhinos exhibits, where people can come and show their love and support for the black rhinos. Two of the biggest zoos to have them include the San Diego Zoo and the St. Louis Zoo. Other majors zoos with black rhinos include the zoos in Denver, Oregon, and Nashville.
